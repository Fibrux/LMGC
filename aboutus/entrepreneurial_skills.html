Beyond Academics

Educating Girls of the 21st Century for a Brighter Tomorrow
An independent girls’ school, La Martiniere Girls' College prepares young women to be self-directed learners and confident leaders. Outside of the classroom, our girls are leaders in every corner of the campus. They never take the back seat and ensure to leave a mark wherever they go. Our girls have learnt the fine art of entrepreneurship and use it for the betterment, and enhancement of society and for the service of humanity. The students learn to become dauntless and self-reliant and never forget to light the lamps of those who don’t have a match.

Isha Zaheer: Fibrux

Fibrux is a digital media services agency offering expert media operations, creative Web Development, as well as ancillary design services to advertisers, publishers and ad tech platforms. Isha founded this company together with a fellow peer from La Martiniere College. Isha has ably led her company over the last two years completing an array of Web Development Projects (including the current School Website) and Graphic Designing Projects.

Nisika Vaish: Ikaya

The name of the brand ‘Ikaya’ means ‘elegant’ and ‘empowerment’. This is an online store where you can find a variety of boho decor,custom handmade jewellery and a lot more! Nisika started out in 2020 and the brand continues to grow every year. Ikaya is currently focusing on their ‘Macrame’ line of home decor and jewellery. Nisika through Ikaya is trying her hardest to retain and continue these rich ancient traditional techniques while keeping up with the modern trends!

Maham Ghazala Tariq: Sweet Story

During Covid-19, Maham decided to turn her hobby of baking into a business. From having her sisters as tasters to baking for a school event for 2000 people, She has grown the revenue of her business by 80% in just the past year itself while managing her board exams simultaneously. Societies, events, residential spaces, orders from all areas of Lucknow, her passion to bake is slowly turning into a big business that she has been dreaming of building ever since she was a young girl.

Aarushi Kapoor: Fluerdelune

Fluerdelune, which means "Moon Flower" in French, is a small business which exists not only as a business but also as a charity organization. It is a handmade jewellery business started and owned by Aarushi Kapoor. The jewellery is made of clay, wire, beads and string, the end products of the same are various, such as rings, necklaces, bracelets, chokers, waist chains, hand chains, earrings and many more. 10% of the profit which is made by the sales is donated to charity.
