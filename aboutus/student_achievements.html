Beyond Academics

Educating Girls of the 21st Century for a Brighter Tomorrow

Tanvi

Tanvi of class 6D struck gold in AR 40 177 Air Rifle(NR) Championship 10m Sub-Youth Women's Individual (under 16) in the 19th Pre UP State Shooting Competition held from 16th May to19thMay,2023, organized at Shree Baldev Singh Shooting Range, Deoband, Saharanpur conducted by UP State Rifle Association, Lucknow. The championship spanning over three days hosted 1500 shooters from all over the State who participated in various categories and also showcased the marksmanship of national-level ace shooters.

Kuhu Kacher

The extraordinary accomplishments of Kuhu Kacher, a brilliant student in class 4, whose writing prowess has propelled her to incredible heights in the literary realm. Over two years, Kuhu has left an indelible mark on the StoryMirror online platform with a staggering portfolio of 50 published English poems! She has two Hindi poems and nine English stories to her name and proves to be a versatile and multifaceted writer. Notably, her exceptional talent earned her a nomination for Author of the Year 2022, competing against writers of all age groups, on an open platform.

Guneet, Aarika and Tiya

The word Marathon calls to mind determination, grit endurance and strength. For most running a marathon is a daunting challenge but not for Guneet, Aarika and Tiya who successfully finished the 3K marathon organised by Team Satyam Shanker Sahai at Ekana Stadium this morning. What is remarkable is that Guneet and Aarika are just 9 years and Tiya only 7! Our respect to these three determined runners! Well done!

Janhavi Shukla

Janhavi Shukla, in class 4, won accolades for her stellar performance in various national and international Olympiads placing her among the top 50 rank holders in the zonal and state-level rankings. She has secured gold and silver medals besides certificates and gifts as rewards for her spectacular achievements in Maths, Science, GK, English and Logical Reasoning. She secured National Rank 61 in the prestigious NSTSE , organised by the Unified Council. She bagged International Rank 2nd in Winter Spell Bee organised by Crest Olympiads.

Adiesha

ADIESHA - OUR YOUNGEST TAEKWONDO CHAMP WON GOLDThe 18th U.P State Taekwondo Championship Poomsae Competition was held on the 5th and 6th of November at the KD Singh Babu Stadium. Adiesha kicked and punched her way to the top winning gold in the Sub Junior category at the State level. Her impressive technique and skill enthralled and captivated all given that Adiesha is just five years of age.

Abigail Deepanjana Lal

Abigail Deepanjana Lal, a class 6 student of LMGC proved her mettle in the 12th Brain-O-Brain Wonderkid 2022 Competition by winning the National Topper trophy while securing a State Rank 9 and National Rank 11 besides a cash prize of Rs2000. The pan-India competition which saw the participation of around 50,000 students from 1500 schools across the nation was designed to motivate, empower and encourage competitive spirit in a limited period of 40 minutes.

Aairah

Aairah, a class 8 student, left her mark on the AITA CS-7 Under 16 tournament in Prayagraj. Amidst fierce competition from players representing 8 states, she claimed victory in the doubles category. In singles, she fought valiantly, securing the runner-up position. Her sensational performance is an inspiration to young athletes, showcasing the power of passion, determination and hard work.

Adhitri Tulsyan

Adhitri Tulsyan, a pupil of class 10 made her mark in the national debating circuit by clinching the runner-up trophy for the prestigious GD Lehmann Inter School English Debate hosted by Wynberg Allen School, Mussoorie , during their annual fest,' le mot juste' on 29th April 2023.She displayed an in-depth analysis and understanding of the topic, 'Mirrors are man's worst invention ' and put forth strong arguments walking away with the runner-up trophy.

Soumya Chandra and Swasti Chandra

The indomitable, dynamic duo of Soumya Chandra of 9D and Swasti Chandra of 6A were unbeatable in the 8th B.S.Sood Memorial Interschool Table Tennis Tournament held at Spring Dale College, Gomti Nagar on the 28th and 29th April 2023 as their spectacular game clinched three titles besides claiming the award for 'Best Participating School'. The strong, smashing shots by the siblings secured them the top spots in both the team event as well as in the singles which saw the two sisters pitted against each other where Soumya Chandra clinched the gold medal while Swasti Chandra bagged the silver. .

Myra Malik

Myra Malik gallops her way into the National Equestrian Show! Winning a medal in an equestrian competition is an achievement that requires hard work, dedication, and a deep bond between the rider and the horse. Myra Malik a pupil of class 7 , secured the 3rd spot in HACKS at the Delhi Horse Show held on 1st April 2023. Her skilful handling of the reins enabled her to clear 10 jumps besides exhibiting her riding prowess in three other events. Clearing her Regional Equestrian Round she earned her place in the National Equestrian Show.

Kopal Saxena and Yashasvi Anika Tandon

The quizzing team of LMGC tightened its grip on the quizzing circuit by making it to the top 6 in the India Quiz (School Level) held at Indira Gandhi Pratishthan on 5th February 2023 as part of the U.P. Global Investors Summit. The team comprising Kopal Saxena of class 9 and Yashasvi Anika Tandon of class 7 was among the 200 teams who were selected through an online round. In the offline preliminary round, our girls matched their wits against 200 teams to qualify directly for the finals as their team was among the top 6 scoring teams.

Aanvi Jain

The mellifluous voice of Aanvi Jain, a pupil of class 7 C won the runner-up trophy in the highly prestigious National level competition INDIAN IDOLS-JUNIORS organized by Round Table India. The event which was judged by the learned and noted faculties of the renowned AR Rehman School of Music and Shankar Mahadevan Academy, Chennai witnessed tough competition among 35 budding singers in the category of 10 -15 years. Aanvi impressed the judges with her vocal skills securing the runners-up trophy.
