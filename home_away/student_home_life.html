Support Weekday Schedule for Boarders
(Lower Prep to Class V)

6:00 - 7:50amThe day begins with bathing & dressing helped by Matron and ayahs followed by breakfast for LP - Class II and for Class 3-5 Team sports followed by breakfast
7:50 - 2:00pmSchool (Academic session)
2:00 - 4:00pmLunch followed by afternoon free for individual needs(baths, laundry, nap time or study etc.)
4:00 - 7:30pmAfternoon tea (& Tuck) followed by study time (homework), Team Sports and organised Games
7:30 - 8:00pmDinner
8:30 - 9:00pmDay Ends (Lights out by 9:00 p.m.)

Saturday Schedule for Boarders
(Lower Prep to Class V)

7am – 8amThe day begins with bathing & dressing helped by Matron and ayahs
8am – 12pmBreakfast followed by free time to play with friends, watch television and movies.
12:pm - 12:30pmLunch
12:30pm to 4:00pmIn dormitories or dressing rooms or watch TV. Children can call their parents through the school phones under matron supervision.
4pm – 4:15pmTea & Tuck time
4:15pm - 6pmPlay outdoors or watch television and movies. Parents and guardians can visit the children
6:15pm –7:15pmTeam sports and organized Games or Play time: Gymnastics, Taekwondo, BasketBall, Swimming, Tennis, Badminton and Roller Skating
7:30pm – 8pmDinner
8pm-9pmIn dormitories or dressing rooms - watch TV or play indoor games e.g. Uno, Monopoly, Snake and Ladders.
9.30pmDay Ends (Lights out by 9:30 p.m)
